Red Flags — 7 Issues Detected
1
Company is NOT a cybersecurity firm
10/10
WebKit24 is a digital marketing & SEO agency (Lucknow, 73 employees). Their LinkedIn confirms services are SEO, Digital Marketing, App Dev — zero cybersecurity operations. Hiring a dedicated security analyst makes no coherent business sense.
2
Wildly conflicting salary data
9/10
LinkedIn posts ₹10K–50K/yr; Jaabz lists $138,550/yr for the same role. A 10× discrepancy signals either data scraping error, intentionally misleading listings, or extremely poor hiring practices.
3
Unrealistic skill stack for 0–2 years
9/10
Demanding CEH, QRadar SIEM, GCP cloud security, penetration testing, NIST framework, IDS/IPS, and full incident response from a fresher is a 4–7 year skill set.
4
Copy-paste job description
8/10
“Monitor security events, analyze risks, and respond to incidents” appears in three nearly identical bullet points. Strong indicator of non-technical staff or AI assembling the JD without review.
5
Severely limited employer transparency
8/10
No Glassdoor page, minimal AmbitionBox reviews, Facebook page shows only 4 reviews and “closed” status. A 2014-founded company with 73 employees having near-zero review footprint is a trust failure.
6
Remote claim unverified
6/10
WebKit24 lists two Lucknow office locations. “Work From Home” listed as a benefit, not confirmed policy. Risk of in-office mandate post-hire.
7
No mention of security team or infrastructure
7/10
A real security role should name the existing SOC setup, SIEM vendor, team size, and reporting line. This JD has none of that — suggesting no existing security infrastructure.

5 Smart Interview Questions
1 WebKit24's website describes you primarily as a digital marketing and SEO agency. Can you walk me through the size of your current security team and the cybersecurity clients this role would support?
2 Which SIEM platform is currently deployed — QRadar, Splunk, or another? And what is the existing alerting infrastructure I'd be monitoring on day one?
3 Different job boards list this role at very different salary ranges. Can you confirm the actual CTC band and what the structured appraisal cycle looks like?
4 The listing says Work From Home — is this fully remote permanently, or is there an expectation to report to the Lucknow office? Is this written into the offer letter?
5 Since CEH and penetration testing are listed as requirements for an entry-level role, does WebKit24 provide study leave, certification reimbursement, or a structured upskilling path?
