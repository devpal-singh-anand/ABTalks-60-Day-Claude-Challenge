## 🚀 Day 25/60 — AI Startup Validation Simulator Rebuild

Today wasn't about building PrepPal AI.

It was about building something that could challenge PrepPal AI. 🦈

While testing my Startup Validation Simulator, I discovered a major flaw:

The platform looked intelligent, but much of the scoring still depended on predefined logic and assumptions.

That meant founders could receive similar outcomes despite giving completely different answers.

So I rebuilt it. ⚙️

### ✨ What's New?

* Dynamic AI-generated questions
* AI-generated follow-up questioning
* Knowledge-base analysis
* Consistency tracking
* Contradiction detection
* Reasoning-based scoring

Instead of rewarding keywords, it now evaluates:

* Clarity
* Consistency
* Realism
* Evidence
* Sustainability Understanding
* Execution Readiness

### 🦈 Real Founder Validation

The most valuable part?

Using it to stress-test PrepPal AI itself.

Hours of founder-reviewer conversations challenged assumptions around:

* Retention
* Sustainability
* Donations
* BYOK Adoption
* Founder Bandwidth
* Long-Term Viability

### 💡 Biggest Lesson

The biggest risk wasn't AI cost.

It was founder discipline and time management.

---

### 🤔 Got a Startup Idea?

Try putting it through the simulator.

Not the polished pitch version.

Not the version you'd post on LinkedIn.

The real version.

The one that gets challenged on:

* Retention
* Sustainability
* Execution
* Differentiation
* Assumptions

You might discover weaknesses you never noticed before.

Or you might walk away with stronger conviction.

Either way, you'll learn something.
