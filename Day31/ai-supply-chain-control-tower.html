❔ Instructions

Objective

Keep the global supply chain running. Maximize your Score and KPIs before the 3-minute shift ends.

KPIs

  • Service Level — orders fulfilled on time.
  • Customer Satisfaction — how happy customers are.
  • Inventory Health — stock accuracy & availability.
  • Transportation Efficiency — fleet & routing performance.
  • Operating Cost — lower is better.
  • Revenue Protected — revenue saved from disruption.

Actions

Each alert offers tailored actions (e.g. Expedite Shipment, Use Backup Supplier, Reroute Trucks). The best-fit action gives the strongest KPI boost; mismatched actions help less or backfire. Ignoring lets an alert expire — sometimes harmless, sometimes costly.
